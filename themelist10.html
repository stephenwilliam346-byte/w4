Sustainable farming practices are crucial for minimizing the environmental impact of meat production. These practices encompass a range of strategies aimed at preserving resources, promoting animal welfare, and enhancing the quality of food produced. Rotational grazing, for example, allows livestock to graze in different areas, promoting soil health and reducing overgrazing. Once livestock is ready for harvest, the next step is processing, which involves transforming the animal into marketable meat products. This stage includes slaughtering, butchering, and packaging.
